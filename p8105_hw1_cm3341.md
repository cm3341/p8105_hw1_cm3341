### Scatterplot of flipper length vs. bill length

``` r
ggplot(
  penguins, aes(
    x = bill_length_mm, 
    y = flipper_length_mm, 
    color = species)) +
  geom_point() +
  labs(
    title = "Flipper Length vs Bill Length", x = "Bill Length (mm)", y = "Flipper Length (mm)") +
  theme_minimal()
```
